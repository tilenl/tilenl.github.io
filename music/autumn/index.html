Inspiration

The idea for the track came in the middle of summer (2020). I was doodling on the piano, when it struck me that the melody invokes a feeling similar to the vibes of autumn. I started expanding the idea, which grew day by day, until a 4 piece album (is it still called an album?) emerged in my mind. It would contain 1 piece for each season: Autumn, Spring, Summer and Winter.


The first of four, Autumn, was made around the end of the year 2020.


Song cover

I used a little trick, to achieve the wanted cover effect. As I come from a more technical background, I knew of a technique called Neural Style Transfer, which was perfect for me and my autumn project.
